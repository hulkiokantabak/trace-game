# COHERENCE MATRIX

## Borges Architects. All Masters Verify.

### Purpose

This matrix ensures architectural integrity: every NPC identity connects to at least one lore fragment and at least one investigation node. Every fragment connects to at least one NPC and one location. No orphans. No contradictions within a single mythology. Contradictions BETWEEN mythologies are deliberate and mapped.

## CONTRADICTION MAP

Deliberate contradictions between mythologies that cannot coexist:

| Contradiction | Mythologies | Nature |
|--------------|-------------|--------|
| London is passive (recording) vs. active (dreaming mind) | Musician vs. Wanderer | Ontological |
| London is self-authoring (palimpsest) vs. collectively constituted (network) | Photographer vs. Barista | Agency |
| The foundation is alive (geological) vs. London is a mind (psychological) | Shopkeeper vs. Wanderer | Category |
| Amplification through attention (Musician) may reassemble what was erased (Barista) | Musician ↔ Barista | Consequential |
| The Watcher was absorbed into sound (M) / saw the revision (P) / was noticed by something vast (W) / erased a name (BA) / felt the ground move (S) | All five | The Watcher is five different people with five different traumas |
| London wants to wake (Wanderer) vs. London should stay asleep (Shopkeeper) | Wanderer vs. Shopkeeper | Ethical |

### The Central Impossibility
All five mythologies agree on one thing: **London is aware of the player.** They disagree on what London is, why it's aware, and what the awareness means. This agreement-in-disagreement is the architecture's deepest joint. The player, regardless of trait, arrives at the same experience — being seen by a city — through five incompatible explanations. The experience is true. The explanations are constructions. The game never says this. The player who plays all five discovers it.
