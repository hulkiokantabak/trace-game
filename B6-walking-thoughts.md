# B6: WALKING THOUGHTS

## Cortázar Leads. Saramago Grounds. Vian Enforces Economy.

### Principles

Walking thoughts appear during between-space transitions and quiet walking moments within neighborhoods. 8 words maximum. They are the player character's interior — not narration, not instruction. A person thinking while walking. The thoughts never explain. They notice.

Tagged: `neutral` (all traits) or trait-specific. Also tagged by: neighborhood transition, time of day, weather, and progression tier.

---

## WITHIN-NEIGHBORHOOD THOUGHTS

### Greenwich

- "The park smells like cut grass and history."
- "Tourists at the Cutty Sark. They don't see."
- "Clocks everywhere. All disagreeing slightly."
- "The bench is empty. Not yet dusk."
- "The meridian line. Everyone stands on it. Grins."

## PROGRESSION-GATED THOUGHTS

These appear only after specific progression milestones.

### After First Forgetting
- "London's quieter today. Missing a frequency."
- "The barista didn't hum this morning."
- "Something's absent. Can't name it yet."

### After Watcher First Noticed
- "That person again. Third time. Not coincidence."
- "Watching me. Or watching what I see."
- "I'm not alone in this. That's not comforting."

### After First Confidant
- "She trusted me. The weight of it."
- "He'd waited years to say that."
- "I know something now I can't unknow."

### After Reaching Awareness Tier 3
- "The layers. The light. All of it."
- "London shows me what others can't see."
- "The city looks back. I'm certain now."

### After Reaching Insight Tier 3
- "I understand more than I want to."
- "The Watcher was right about some of it."
- "Not what's happening. What I do about it."
