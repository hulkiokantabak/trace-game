## STAT PROGRESSION CURVES

### Core Stats: Awareness, Connection, Insight

Each stat has an internal value (0-100) invisible to the player. The player sees three vertical bars in the journal — no numbers. The bars are divided into three tiers.

**Tier thresholds:**
- Tier 1: 0-30 (newcomer)
- Tier 2: 31-65 (established)
- Tier 3: 66-100 (deep)

### XP Sources and Rates

**Awareness XP (→ Awareness stat):**

| Action | XP | Frequency |
|--------|----|-----------|
| Visit a new location for the first time | 5 | Once per location (30 max) |
| Discover a hidden detail | 3 | Multiple per location, trait-gated |
| Find a lore fragment | 8 | 7 per playthrough |
| Walk a between-space for the first time | 3 | Once per route (3 max) |
| Visit a location in new weather/time condition | 1 | Repeatable, diminishing |
| Notice the Watcher in the background | 10 | Once |
| Discover a hidden location | 10 | 2-3 per playthrough |

Approximate sessions to Tier 2: 15-20. To Tier 3: 40-50.

**Connection XP (→ Connection stat):**

| Action | XP | Frequency |
|--------|----|-----------|
| First meeting with an NPC | 3 | Once per NPC (22 max) |
| NPC advances to Acquaintance | 5 | Per NPC |
| NPC advances to Familiar | 10 | Per NPC |
| NPC advances to Confidant | 20 | Per NPC |
| NPC advances to Mythological Ally | 30 | 2-3 per playthrough |
| Share a meal/drink with an NPC | 2 | Repeatable, once per NPC per week |
| Choose to share information with an NPC | 3 | Per investigation choice |
| AI conversation that deepens relationship | 1 | Per meaningful exchange, CAPPED at 5 per NPC per session |

Approximate sessions to Tier 2: 20-25. To Tier 3: 50-60.

**Insight XP (→ Insight stat):**

| Action | XP | Frequency |
|--------|----|-----------|
| Resolve a Quick investigation node | 5 | Per node |
| Advance a Deep investigation node (per step) | 3 | Per step |
| Resolve a Deep investigation node | 15 | Per node |
| Connect two lore fragments thematically | 10 | Rare, player-initiated |
| Experience The Forgetting actively | 5 | Weekly |
| Confront the Watcher | 20 | Once |
| Reach a Climactic node | 25 | 1-2 per playthrough |

Approximate sessions to Tier 2: 25-30. To Tier 3: 55-65.

### Trait-Specific Stat

Each trait has one additional stat (Resonance, Clarity, Attunement, Threads, Depth) with the same 0-100 scale and tier structure. It grows through trait-aligned actions:

**Musician — Resonance:** Grows from hearing anomalous sounds, investigating sound-related nodes, reaching NPC musical identities, finding Musician fragments.

**Photographer — Clarity:** Grows from spotting visual anomalies, examining details at locations, investigating visual nodes, finding Photographer fragments.

**Wanderer — Attunement:** Grows from following the pull, visiting locations based on feeling, investigating emotional nodes, finding Wanderer fragments.

**Barista — Threads:** Grows from cross-referencing NPC information, connecting people, investigating relational nodes, finding Barista fragments.

**Shopkeeper — Depth:** Grows from identifying anachronisms, comparing present to past, investigating temporal nodes, finding Shopkeeper fragments.

## UNLOCK SCHEDULE

### Awareness Unlocks

| Tier | What Opens |
|------|-----------|
| 1 (0-30) | Basic trait-gated details visible. Surface lore fragments accessible. Standard investigation nodes available. |
| 2 (31-65) | Hidden locations discoverable (L10 empty lot, B08 rooftop). Deeper details appear at visited locations (retroactive discovery). The Watcher becomes noticeable in background scenes. Tier 2 lore fragments accessible. Nocturnal content expanded. |
| 3 (66-100) | The Watcher confrontable. Most concealed content available. Mythological layer vivid and undeniable. Tier 3 lore fragments (the deepest, most disturbing). Climactic nodes accessible. |

### Connection Unlocks

| Tier | What Opens |
|------|-----------|
| 1 | NPCs progress to Acquaintance. Surface investigation content via NPCs. |
| 2 | Familiar and Confidant stages accessible. NPCs share backstories. Flat accumulates NPC gifts. NPC-directed investigation paths open. The first mythological identity clues become visible. |
| 3 | Mythological Ally stage. Select NPCs actively participate. Deepest NPC secrets. NPC-initiated encounters. Cross-NPC investigation paths. |

### Insight Unlocks

| Tier | What Opens |
|------|-----------|
| 1 | Lore fragments interpretable. Basic investigation nodes resolvable. |
| 2 | Complex investigation nodes resolvable. Player can act during The Forgetting (noticing things invisible in vivid states). Main storyline midpoint accessible. Leave London ending unlocked. |
| 3 | Event endings available. Climactic investigation nodes accessible. The Watcher's full story available. The deepest mythology. |

### Trait Stat Unlocks

| Tier | Effect |
|------|--------|
| 1 | Trait-specific details faintly present. Mythology suggestive — could be coincidence. |
| 2 | New sensory details appear at already-visited locations (retroactive discovery). Mythology persistent — hard to dismiss. NPC trait-identities show stronger clues. |
| 3 | Mythology vivid. The player lives between two Londons. The sprite's highest evolution. Full trait-specific investigation content. |

## TITLE SYSTEM

Titles: 2 words maximum. Rare. 25-30 total. A player earns 8-12 per playthrough. Displayed to others via residuum marks and agent journals.

### Universal Titles

| Title | Condition |
|-------|-----------|
| Night-Walker | Visit all 30 locations between midnight and 4 AM |
| Tide-Reader | Find all tidal foreshore objects |
| Fog-Finder | Discover a hidden location during fog |
| Rain-Keeper | Play 10 sessions during rain |
| Three-Borough | Visit all three neighborhoods in a single session |
| Dream-Touched | Experience a Bright Tide at Awareness Tier 3 |
| Still-Watcher | Experience a Still Tide without visiting any NPC |
| Watcher-Known | Confront the Watcher |
| Home-Maker | Accumulate 15+ objects in the flat |
| Long-Resident | Play for 60+ real days |

### Connection Titles

| Title | Condition |
|-------|-----------|
| Named | An NPC uses your character's name for the first time (title records which NPC) |
| Trusted | Reach Confidant with 5+ NPCs |
| Tea-Shared | Accept a meal/drink from 10 different NPCs |
| Silence-Keeper | Choose [Say nothing] in 5 conversation choices |
| Ally-Found | Reach Mythological Ally with any NPC |

### Trait Titles (3-5 per trait)

**Musician:** Frequency-Finder (hear the canal note at full Resonance), Archive-Listener (find all 7 Musician fragments), Song-Keeper (preserve the barista's melody), Note-Reader (connect 3 Musician fragments thematically)

**Photographer:** Layer-Reader (identify 3 visual palimpsest effects), Shadow-Mapper (document St Anne's impossible shadow), Draft-Seer (see London's next version at full Clarity), Frame-Holder (find all 7 Photographer fragments)

**Wanderer:** Pull-Follower (follow the emotional pull to 5 locations), Dream-Walker (enter the dream cartography), Edge-Keeper (guard the dream without entering), Drift-Finder (find all 7 Wanderer fragments)

**Barista:** Web-Seer (perceive the full network at high Threads), Name-Keeper (learn the erased name), Bridge-Builder (connect 3 NPCs who didn't know each other), Thread-Counter (find all 7 Barista fragments)

**Shopkeeper:** Deep-Knower (touch the foundation stone), Mark-Finder (locate all 7 foundation marks), Time-Keeper (identify 5 anachronisms), Ground-Reader (find all 7 Shopkeeper fragments)

## THE FLAT — Object Accumulation Schedule

Objects appear tied to story beats. Each is a pixel art item rendered in the flat scene.

| # | Trigger | Object | Placement |
|---|---------|--------|-----------|
| 1 | First NPC reaches Acquaintance | A note or card from the NPC | Table, leaning against the notebook |
| 2 | First lore fragment found | The fragment, handwritten on paper | Pinned to wall above table |
| 3 | First investigation choice made | An object from the investigation (trait-specific) | Shelf |
| 4 | First Forgetting experienced | A mark on the window — condensation pattern that dried strangely | Window |
| 5 | Third NPC reaches Acquaintance | A small gift — a button, a pen, a sketch | Table |
| 6 | First season change experienced | A seasonal element — dried leaf, snowflake impression, wildflower | Window ledge |
| 7 | First Confidant NPC | A personal item from that NPC | Prominent — wall hook or shelf center |
| 8 | First hidden location discovered | A fragment from that place — a stone, a tile chip, a feather | Shelf |
| 9 | The Watcher first noticed | A photograph on the table the player didn't take — of themselves, from behind | Table, face down |
| 10 | Main storyline midpoint | The key investigation object (trait-specific) | Central position |
| 11 | Second season change | Another seasonal element | Window ledge, beside the first |
| 12 | Mythological Ally reached | An object that bridges the real and mythological — given by the ally NPC | Beside the bed |
| 13 | Fourth lore fragment | Additional fragment pinned to wall | Wall, creating a small collection |
| 14 | The Watcher confronted | The Watcher's object — something they left behind | Table, beside the photograph |
| 15 | Climactic node reached | The final investigation object | The most prominent position in the flat |
| 16+ | Additional fragments, NPC gifts, seasonal markers | Various | Filling the remaining space |

By endgame, the flat should feel full — not cluttered, but lived-in. Every object is a memory. The player opens the game, sees their flat, and sees their story.

**Visual Grid (Mobile):** Flat objects occupy fixed positions — 4 on the shelf, 4 on the wall, 4 on the table, 2 on the window ledge, 2 by the bed. Positions are visible from session 1 as empty spaces that gradually fill. Objects appear in order. The filling of the grid IS the visual progression.

## EXPERIENCE DISPLAY

The journal's stats page shows:

```
[Character sprite — current evolution stage]
[Trait name and icon]

AWARENESS   ████████░░░░ [Tier 2]
CONNECTION  █████░░░░░░░ [Tier 1]  
INSIGHT     ██████████░░ [Tier 2]
[TRAIT STAT] ███████░░░░░ [Tier 2]

Days in London: 34
Places Known: 22/30
People Known: 14/22
Mysteries: 4 resolved, 3 active, 2 undiscovered
Fragments: 5/7
Titles: Night-Walker, Named (by the Barista), Fog-Finder
```

No numbers visible — only the bars and the counts. The bars fill smoothly, not in steps. The player sees gradual growth, not level-ups.

**Journal Tabs (Mobile):** Five tabs along the bottom of the notebook: **People | Places | Mysteries | Lore | Me**. "Me" combines stats, titles, and choice history. One tap, one page. No nested menus. Swiping changes tabs.
