## 1. THE CLOCKMAKER

**Wanderer:** The location chose him, not the reverse. He never leaves during working hours. His breathing slows at the back of the shop. He sometimes pauses and stares at the wall as if listening beyond it. | Confidant: "I tried to retire. Got to Paddington. My feet wouldn't go." | W-F4

**Shopkeeper:** The latest in a line of clockmakers since 1780. The shop has never closed. Tools too old for current use, polished and maintained. A list of previous proprietors behind the counter.
